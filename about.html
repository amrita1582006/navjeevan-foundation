Who We Are

Divine Help Foundation is here to help you or your loved one

Addiction to alcohol or drugs is not just a bad habit—it is a serious mental health disorder that affects a person’s thoughts, emotions, and behaviour. Many individuals feel helpless against addiction, but with proper medical care, counselling, and therapy, recovery is completely possible. Divine Help Foundation is a trusted rehabilitation centre in Delhi NCR dedicated to helping individuals regain control of their lives through compassionate and professional treatment. Our experienced therapists, peaceful environment, personalized recovery programs, one-on-one counselling, group therapy sessions, and holistic healing approach help individuals achieve long-term sobriety, mental wellness, and a healthier, more positive future.

Our Mission

Divine Help Foundation Mission

Our mission is to deliver excellent care and cure with uncompromised quality and time-tested treatment at our rehab home. We provide treatment with compassion and empathy so that your loved one can live a life of dignity and self respect free from all sought of addiction be it alcoholeism or drug abuse.

Our Vision

Divine Help Foundation Vision

Our vision is to establish a network of rehab homes in cities throught India

Our vision is to build a society where people facing addiction, anxiety or emotional struggles can receive timely help without fear, shame or confusion. We aim to create long-term recovery through awareness, family involvement, professional care and continued support after treatment.
